Dealing with pests is not that of an easy task, but you getting into this "site" you have made a great choice. It is hard but all comes down to education, the trouble is that the Traditional method is outdated and confuses people. Despite the availability of information about pests on the internet, too often this is written by people with no professional connection or hands on experience of the pests they are discussing. This wastes time, money and hands advantage back to the pests!

Discovering advice or "information" that solves is the main reason your in this site, Knowledge is the key to developing a better understanding of pest activity and good housekeeping can often solve a pest problem just as surely as wise use of pesticides will.
